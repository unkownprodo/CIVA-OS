# CIVA-OS

Coordinate-Free AI Evaluation Framework

CIVA-OS is a research proposal for evaluating AI systems without relying on fixed latent coordinate spaces or benchmark score vectors.

Instead of projecting models into an arbitrary embedding space, CIVA-OS models the AI population as a relational system and measures structural change through intrinsic invariants.

Core concepts include:

- Pairwise behavioral interaction graphs
- Persistent homology
- Graph curvature
- Gromov-Wasserstein geometry
- Adaptive verifier evolution
- Coordinate-free metrology
- Self-auditing measurement systems

## Motivation

Traditional benchmarks depend on fixed evaluation axes.

If the evaluator changes, or if the capability landscape evolves, benchmark scores may drift for reasons unrelated to the models themselves.

CIVA-OS explores whether AI evaluation can instead be grounded in intrinsic geometric and topological structure.
